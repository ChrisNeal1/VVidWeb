Welcome to Verbatim Video


To optimize our video/audio expertise and translate it into effective products for our clients to use through trial. Special products are welcome and custom tailored to fit budget and need. We value all of our business relationships and try to cultivate them into lifelong bonds.

Services List:


    Deposition Recordings

    A professional, trained videographer will record a deposition with appropriate equipment in accordance to local rules, as well as the Federal Rule of Civil Procedure.

    Trial Editing

    The segments you designate will be custom edited to your specifications. This process will allow distracting objections to be removed.

    Video Syncronization

    Encoded digital video is synchronized to the court reporter's transcript. Thus enabling a person to simultaneously watch and read testimony. Testimony will be word searchable. Clips can also be created and easily exported to most trail presentation software.

    Site Inspections

    The use of portable cameras lets you take the jury to the scene. Our expertise covers construction sites, automobiles, airplanes, ships, structurally damaged buildings and more. Still photography is also available for greater detail.

    Multi-Camera Mock Trials

    Use our two camera system to record your mock trial. See the jury and the podium in review. A split-screen will be provided upon request.

    Witness Evaluation

    Video recording a witness pretrial may give insight to a person's behavioral responses.
